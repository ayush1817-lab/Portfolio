I spent **3 years as a game designer and 3D animator**, crafting immersive worlds and bringing characters to life.

But I kept asking one question: *"What problem does this actually solve?"*

That question pulled me toward **product design**, where craft meets purpose. Currently, I am designing AI systems for healthcare that feel human. I bring the same attention to detail as an animator, but channel it into systems that genuinely help people.
